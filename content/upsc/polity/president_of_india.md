# President of India (Article 52-62)

## Key Facts
* **Head of State:** First citizen of India.
* **Election:** Indirect election (Electoral College).
* **Term:** 5 Years.

## Eligibility
1. Citizen of India.
2. Minimum age: 35 years.
3. Qualified for election as a member of the Lok Sabha.

> **Exam Tip (SSC CGL):** Article 61 deals with the Impeachment of the President.
